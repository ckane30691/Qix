This is a take on Qix (pronounced "kicks"), an arcade game released by Taito America Corporation in 1981. The objective of Qix is to fence off, or claim, a supermajority of the playfield.

2 / 5

Use the W A S D keys to move. Press the space bar to draw lines.

3 / 5

Connect your line to the edge of the board, the start point of your line, or existing captured areas.

4 / 5

Capture 75% of the board and you win!

5 / 5

Watch for enemies though, if they hit you or the incomplete line you're drawing, it's game over.
